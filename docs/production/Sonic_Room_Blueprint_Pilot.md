## 1. Spatial Architecture (The "Rooms")
The episode is structured into distinct sonic environments that reflect the "Realness Spectrum" of the nine voices.

| Room ID | Environment | Voice(s) | Sonic Signature |

## 2. Sound Design Palette (The "Threads")

### A. The "Audit" Motif (The Mechanical Shame)
*   **Teletype Machine:** Represents the bureaucratic machinery of the coup. Starts rhythmic, ends in "dead silence."
*   **Pen Clicking:** Specifically for Dr. Voss. Two clicks: one for the question, one for the realization.
*   **Slide Projector:** Used in the aftermath to signify the "archival" nature of the tragedy.

### B. The "Siphon" Motif (The Resource Theft)
*   **Fluid Dynamics:** Low-frequency "hum" of oil moving through pipes.
*   **Desert Wind:** A cold, rising wind that signifies the transition from Iranian prosperity to the "blowback" era.
*   **Morse Code:** Faint, high-frequency undertone during the "Operation" segment (SR-04).

### C. The "Bell" (The Moral Anchor)
*   **The Cathedral Bell:** A low, resonant tone that strikes once at key pivots (e.g., after Diana Cross's intro and after Dr. Voss's "They overthrew a democracy").

## 3. Character Vocal Profiles (The "Frequencies")

| Character | Tone | Mic Placement | Processing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Diana Cross** | Broadcast-precise | 4-6 inches | Slight compression, "Newsroom" EQ (clean highs). |
| **Solomon Grey** | Deep baritone | 8-10 inches | Natural room reverb, warm mids. |
| **Malik Barnes** | Low, deliberate | 2-3 inches | High proximity effect, raw/unprocessed. |
| **Dr. Elena Voss** | Clipped, clinical | 4 inches | Very dry, gate applied to remove all room noise. |
| **Zora Flame** | Whispered/Rhythmic | 1-2 inches | Intimate, high-end "air" boost, soft limiting. |
| **Cash Morrow** | Sardonic | 6-8 inches | "Bar" filter (slight low-pass), casual off-axis feel. |
| **Rev. Stone** | Sermon cadence | 12+ inches | Large hall reverb, dynamic range left wide. |

## 4. Production Directives (The "CEL" Alignment)

1.  **The "Crude" Transition:** During Zora Flame's segment, the sound of oil siphoning should slowly morph into the sound of a heartbeat, then fade into silence.
2.  **The "Roosevelt" Suitcase:** A specific foley sound of a heavy suitcase being set down during the "Operation" segment to represent the $1M bribe money.
3.  **The "Gatekeeper" Static:** Use radio static transitions between segments to remind the listener that this is a "broadcast from the gate" that wasn't supposed to be heard.
